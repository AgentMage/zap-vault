# Spirit

![[File0364.jpg]]

The spiritual realm is available right now. The Babylon Matrix focuses our attention on surfaces and appearances, but the spiritual dimension is always present. 

Pause the busyness, take a few deep breaths, and tune into the spiritual dimension. How do various elements in your life appear from the spiritual perspective? Does the spiritual realm have something to say to you? Give it  space to speak. By consulting an oracle, you are opening a channel to the spiritual realm. Take time to contemplate what it has to say and integrate that into your life.
