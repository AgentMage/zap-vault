# Positive Trickster

![[File0369.jpg]]

[WATCH THIS CARD AS A VIDEO](https://youtu.be/E-4w-l0Tcps)

This card indicates an auspicious time for positive trickster energy. 

When you seek to progress, you inevitably hit walls blocking your path. Use positive trickster energy -- imagination, creativity, cleverness, adaptability, serendipity, guile, humor, and inspired improvisation to see around corners and wile your way around negative persons, bureaucracies, businesses, petty authorities, and other obstructive forces. 

If you significantly compromise your ethics while doing so, you are into dark trickster territory.
