# Excess Caution

![[wolfcaution.jpg]]

[WATCH THIS CARD AS A VIDEO](https://youtu.be/aJx1VkIoKfo)

Excess caution can lead to stagnation. Beware an excessively cautionary ego in yourself or others that would keep you from taking necessary risks. Life is intrinsically risky, and physical security is always an illusion. Just as a lack of caution can be anti-life, so too can excess caution. Consider this an auspicious time to take the risks you need to engage with life fully.
