# Unbalanced Vision

![[seatll.jpg]]

You can't see clearly while you are off balance. Don't make long-term choices until you have regained a calm, clear center. Don't take actions of great consequence while in a state of agitation or inner chaos. Wait for clarity to return.

---

If emotional agitation or negative thought loops are skewing your perceptions see [A Guide to the Perplexed Interdimensional Traveler](http://www.zaporacle.com/textpattern/article/11/a-guide-to-the-perplexed-interdimensional-traveler-)
