# Working with/ Transforming the Materials at Hand

![[File0254.jpg]]

[WATCH THIS CARD AS A VIDEO](https://youtu.be/DIE5afs9rKw)
"An inventive approach to using the materials at hand is a hallmark of survivor thinking."
 -- Laurence Gonzalez, *Deep Survival*

You have everything you need in the present situation to work with impeccably. Working toward manifesting additional resources and opportunities could be warranted, but the present situation supplies you with everything you need to take the next step. Think out of the box -- people have changed the world with nothing more than pen and paper. You can create magic from mundane materials if you creatively embrace what is before you.
