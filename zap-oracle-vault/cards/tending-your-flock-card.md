# Tending your Flock

![[File0262.jpg]]

[WATCH THIS CARD AS A VIDEO](https://youtu.be/erkDzuCAvyo)

Woe to the shepherd who abandons their flock. 

Tend to what is in your care -- your body and psyche, those to whom you are connected by inner ties or are otherwise responsible, and your life mission. 

Consider this an auspicious time to protect and nurture all you are responsible for.
