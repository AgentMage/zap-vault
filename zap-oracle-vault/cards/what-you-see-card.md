# What you See

![[File0107.jpg]]

What you see may not be what others see. Remember that you are the star of your movie, while other people are the stars of their movies; therefore, the same scene looks different from another set of eyes. Also, what people see is based on their level of development. As someone once pointed out, "When a pickpocket looks at a saint, he sees only his pockets."
