# Tower of Babel

![[File0335.jpg]]

This card may indicate a massive ego communication collapse that could be intrapsychic, interpersonal and/or collective. 

Ego and mind often try to communicate without being guided by the global intuition of the[Self](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Self_in_Jungian_psycholog), and that leads to ungrounded, unstable structures of thought bound to collapse.

 If one or more parties involved in interpersonal communication have this sort of inner communication, then interpersonal communication will be similarly limited and unstable. 

The positive aspect is that this may be an auspicious time to work on effective communication -- intrapsychically and interpersonally.
