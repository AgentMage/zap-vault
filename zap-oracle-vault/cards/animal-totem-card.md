# Animal Totem

![[File0095.jpg]]

Find your animal totem. What animal essences do you need to access at this phase of your life? 

A young woman once came to me with a dream that involved an elk and asked, "What do native Americans say an elk means?" I told her it was more relevant what an elk means to her. I asked her what qualities came to mind when she looked at an elk with her mind's eye.

Which animals seem numinous or seem to be showing up synchronistically in your life? 

This card also means to ask of each thing, person, or choice what is it in its essence?
