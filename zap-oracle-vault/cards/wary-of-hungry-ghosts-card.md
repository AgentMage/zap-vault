# Wary of Hungry Ghosts

![[halloween08 004.jpg]]

This card warns you about parasitism and morbid, ravenous appetites. This could involve aspects of yourself and/or of others persons or entities. A life that is not driven by meaning, by a core of self-actualization and the will to help others, quickly devolves into the hungry ghost lifestyle where one is forever looking for the next buzz, the next rush, hottie, payout, whatever, to fill that vast, bored, inner emptiness that can never be sated. The positive aspect is that this is a propitious time to work on that.
