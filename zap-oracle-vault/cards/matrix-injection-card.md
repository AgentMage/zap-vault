# Matrix Injection

![[File0259.jpg]]

[WATCH THIS CARD AS VIDEO](https://youtu.be/H07N9V5di98)

Be wary of Matrix Injections -- toxic foods, chemicals, social transactions, memes, and ideologies before they enter the gate of change and become part of you.  

Are there any poisons you allow into your body? Every time you eat junk food or allow other known toxins into your body or mind, you are submitting to Matrix Injection.

The most obvious form of matrix injection is the modern tendency to eat food-like substances composed of highly manipulated ingredients. Another form of matrix injection is promiscuity, an exchange of vital energy with strangers. Just as the exchange of bodily fluids can be dangerous on the microbiological plane, the careless exchange of essential energy can be just as dangerous on other levels. As the alchemical motto reminds us, "As above, so below." 

When it comes to pharmaceuticals such as vaccines, don't be a for-or-against absolutist. Pharmaceuticals can kill you or save your life and every sort of outcome in between. Vet medications on a case-by-case basis using credible-source research and risk/benefit analysis. 

We are constantly receiving Matrix Injections of cultural memes. For example, social engineers create Matrix Injections called "ads,"  which they cleverly design to manipulate your feelings and behavior. Advertisements inject you with thought forms that influence you to believe that certain objects or services are necessary for your happiness or survival. Social media algorithms inject you with arousal-addicting memes to hold your attention for the benefit of social media corporations. Malign persons and organizations can use any art form or means of cultural transmission as a vector for Matrix Injections. Be careful about which cultural products you choose to consume. 

On the microbiological plane, we cannot survive without an immune system that includes an army of white blood cells that defend the citadel of the body from an environment rampant with dangerous microorganisms. Similarly, you need to have cultural and interpersonal immune systems that protect you from an environment that abounds with toxic cultural memes and social transactions. Just as with the microbiological plane, it is neither practical nor desirable to put yourself in a sterile environment to cope with the hazards. Children raised in overly sanitary environments are more likely to suffer from asthma. You need to have strong immune systems that can cope with microbes and Matrix Injections. 

Be your own alchemist, carefully guarding your inner cauldron and exercising discernment and caution about what foods, pharmaceuticals, human energies, and cultural memes are allowed to enter.
