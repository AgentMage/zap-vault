# Fools Rush In

![[april08 044.jpg]]

Fools rush in where angels fear to tread. Respect appropriate boundaries. There is a difference between pushing the envelope and stepping off a cliff. Boundaries are often what protect us from losing our power and life energy. Especially important are the boundaries suggested by your global intuition. As Goethe said, "A master first reveals himself in his ability to hold back."
