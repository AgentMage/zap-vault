# Divine Energy

![[File0322.jpg]]

A spectrum of energies is available to you, from the divine to the mundane and inferior. Often, it is a question of what you choose to tune into. 

The internet is a familiar example. There are a million porno sites you could have visited, but instead, you chose to interact with this oracle. You can easily find the worst of the worst on the internet, or you can explore [The William Blake Archive](http://www.blakearchive.org/blake/) (the visionary artist who created this image). 

Divine energy is no more hidden than sunlight or starlight. It is everywhere, and you can find it in yourself as well. What sort of energy are you seeking? Consider this an auspicious time to choose the highest option.
