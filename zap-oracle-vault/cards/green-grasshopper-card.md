# Green Grasshopper

![[File0363.jpg]]

Accessing green grasshopper life energy.

Vitality arises from various sources. The most primary source is meaningfulness -- the core enthusiasm we have for doing something purposeful with our lives. 

During corporeal incarnation, our vitality largely depends on how we treat our bodies. Grasshoppers are herbivores that can jump the human equivalent of twenty-five feet high and one hundred feet long. Eating a diet that emphasizes whole foods like fruits and vegetables is a way to boost vitality for many people. Cardio exercise can also dramatically boost vitality.

Eliminating or reducing all the things that drain your vitality is another way to access green grasshopper life energy.

Consider this an auspicious time to boost your vitality.
