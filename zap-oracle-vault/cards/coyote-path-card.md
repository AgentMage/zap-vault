# Coyote Path

![[File0327.jpg]]

Warily, intuitively, shrewdly sensing the path just up ahead. Coyotes are famous as tricksters, and adaptation right now might require that you be a positive trickster. Use your intuition to find a way around the walls that block you. Sniff out situations and people before they get too close. Sense what's going to happen before it exits the gate of change.
